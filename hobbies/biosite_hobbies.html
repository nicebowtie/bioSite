Sarah's 'Nuts & Bolts' Chex Mix™ Recepie

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 sticks of butter
  • 2 tsp of Lawry’s Seasoned Salt
  • 6 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 cups Wheat Chex
  • 2 cups Rice Chex
  • 2 cups Corn Chex
  • 2 cups Cheerios
  • 1 (12oz) container mixed nuts (2 1/2 cups approx.)
  • 1-2 cups of pretzel sticks

Directions:

  • Makes 10 cups
  • Preheat oven to 250 degrees
  • Melt butter in a large roasting pan
  • Remove, stir in salt and Worcestershire
  • Gradually add cereal & nuts, then coat
  • Bake 1 hour at 250 degrees stirring every 15 minutes
  • Pour on paper towels to drain

Enjoy!
